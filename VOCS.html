A motor vehicle service or tune-up is a series of maintenance procedures carried out at a set time interval or after the vehicle has traveled a certain distance. This kind of services will be provided by our quality team at your step.


Services Description:

• Engine Oil change.
• Oil Filter replacement.
• Air Filter Cleaning or replacement.
• Radiator Coolant Check and TOPUP.
• Break Oil check and TOPUP.
• Vehicle Body Wash.
• Inside Cabin Air blow cleaning.
• Tyres wash.

Complementary:


• Battery water Check and TOPUP.
• Dashboard polish
• Air inflation check and update.
• Wiper washer water check and TOPUP.

Financials:


• All above mentioned services will be cost @ 1500 PKR (Without taxes) for all types of passenger vehicles excluding HIACE and BUS types of vehicle.
• For Commercial vehicles including HIACE BUS and Mini trucks will be cost @ 2000 (Without taxes) PKR.

Note:


• Engine Oil, Oil Filter, Break Oil and Air Filter cost will be charged as per brand and product price.
• The above-mentioned price is exclusive of any kind of taxes.
